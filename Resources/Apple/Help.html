Stats

Strength - Increases health and attack.

Vitality - Increases health and defense.

Agility - Increases attack, defense, evade, and speed.

Wisdom - Increases special and critical.

Luck - Increases health, attack, defense, special, critical, evade, and speed.

Health - The health stat is how much damage can be taken before being defeated.

Health = Strength * 25 + Vitality * 50 + Luck * 5

Attack - Normal damage is in the range of the attack stat.

Min Attack = Strength * 5 + Luck * 1

Max Attack = Strength * 8 + Agility * 2 + Luck * 1

Defense - All damage is reduced by the defense stat.

Min Defense = Vitality * 2 + Luck * 1

Max Defense = Vitality * 3 + Agility * 1 + Luck * 1

Special - Usable powers, critical attacks, and evasion use the special stat to increase or decrease the damage.

Min Special = Wisdom * 5 + Luck * 1

Max Special = Wisdom * 9 + Agility * 1 + Luck * 1

Critical - The percentage chance to add the special stat to damage.

Critical % = Wisdom / 18 + Luck / 6

Evade - The percentage chance to decrease damage received by the special stat.

Evade % = Agility / 7 + Luck / 12

Speed - The percentage increase until the units next turn (from 0.3 seconds to 1.8 seconds). Speeds above 100% increase Attack, Defense, and Special.

Speed % = Agility / 6 + Luck / 18

Shops

Uncommon Shop - Gives access to tier 2 scrolls and rings are 10% stronger.

Rare Shop - Gives access to tier 2 and 3 scrolls and rings are 25% stronger.

Training - Permanently increase stats. The more you train a specific stat, the more expensive training that stat becomes. Overall training becomes more expensive also.
